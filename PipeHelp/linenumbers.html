Line Numbers

Pipe can display line numbers for each line in your script to make it easier for you to find errors.

  • To toggle line numbers, choose “Edit” > “Line Numbers”.
  • To jump to a certain line number, choose “Edit” > “Find” > “Go to Line…”
    You can use this command even if the line numbers are not currently visible.

Whether line numbers are visible or not is a setting that applies to all of your scripts. Pipe does not save this setting individually for each script.
